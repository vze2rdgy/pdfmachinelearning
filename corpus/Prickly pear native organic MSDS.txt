\FAM[FIRST AID  MEASURES
Excessive inhalation : breath on fresh air. In case of sickness, seek medical advice.
Skin  contact : wash  abundantly  with soap  and  water. Remove  contaminated  clothing. In  case  of  persistent  cutaneous reactions, seek medical advice.
Eye contact :Rinse abundantly with water or an eyewash solution for at least 15 minutes holding the eyelids open and if irritation persists, seek medical advice.
Ingestion : Rinse the mouth out with water. Do not induce vomiting, and if you feel unwell, seek medical advice.In general, in case of uncertainly or if symtoms are shown, always seek medical advice\]

\FIR[MEASURES IN CASE OF FIRE
Suitable extinguishing media :Carbon dioxide, water foam, dry powders. 
Special risks :None
Special  protective  equipment  :Put  on  a  mask  to  avoid  breathing  fumes  or  emitted  vapour  in  case  of  unsufficient ventilation. 
Cool down the containers with pulverized water from a secure distance\]

\SPL[MEASURES BY ACCIDENTAL SPILLAGE
Individual precautions : isolate the zone. Do not inhale the vapours.  Use in a well-ventilated room. Wear gloves and safety goggles when handling leakages. Follow general hygiene procedure in case of accidental spillage.
Enironmental precautions : Avoid all ground and surface water contamination. Control the product by covering with an inert absorbent substance. If the product contaminates into water, river or swearage systems, warns the local authorities according to regulation procedures.
Cleaning procedures :Collect with absorbent materials.Clean the floor preferably with water and/or detergent\]

\STR[HANDLING  AND  STORAGE
Handling :Use in proper ventilated areas. 
Storage  :It  is  recommended  to  store  the  product  in  original  sealed  and  full  containers  in  a  well-ventilated  area,  away from the light and from sources of heat. Storageat room temperature (22Â°C)\]
